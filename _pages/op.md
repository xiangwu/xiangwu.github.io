ASIR **Open Platform** has three main catagaries: 

## ASIR-GURU
ASIR-Guru is an Open Platform is a hierarchical collection of open source projcet.
It inlcudes three types of creative projects, include RoboZoo, VisualGarden and EasyHelper.

You can find them [here](https://github.com/xiangwu/ASIR_GURU)

## ROSE
Robotics Study Environment(ROSE) is a learning and simulation Environment based on Matlab and CoppeliaSim, includes several open course and tutorials with matierials and exercises.

You can find them [here](https://github.com/xiangwu/ROSE)


## CVSE
Computer Vision Study Environment(CVSE) is a learning and test platform based on Python, which includes several open courses and tutorials with matierials and exercises. 

You can find them [here](https://github.com/xiangwu/CVSE)
